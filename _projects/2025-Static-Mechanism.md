For a class project, we were tasked with designing and modeling a lifting mechanism within a fixed 2D design space of 150 cm by 50 cm. The goal was to create a frame that could lift the maximum possible weight to the highest possible height using one rigid bar, three pin supports (two grounded), and a single linear actuator chosen from an online catalog.

My final design had a 150 cm bar that was pinned to a linear actuator. That was supported by a 50 cm bar that was pinned to the ground.
